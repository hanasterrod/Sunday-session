About ZenTask

ZenTask was created to go from stress to sucess. Our uers will be able to use the app to track their welness journey. The app offers users to choose self guided or guided meditation sessions, add daily task's for them to complete, add journal entries, track their mood, and set reminders.

Why choose ZenTask?

ZenTask is not only to help a user become more productive but also proactive with their welness journey. It keeps users accountable and be able to look back on how much they have progressed.

Our Mission

The world we live in today is a fast paced world, daily many people experience anxiety and high levels of stress. People are always looking for new ways to help improve their mental health and tune into their mind by creating daily routines to help reduce all of the daily pressures. Many people that are looking for help don't know where to start their journey
